Frequent questions about limescale protection & water filters

Everything you want to know about pure water.

Does TESL® II limescale protection really work without chemicals and salt?

Yes. TESL® II changes the crystal structure of the limescale purely physically so that it no longer adheres. No chemicals or salts are added, and the valuable minerals stay in your drinking water.

Do the healthy minerals stay in the drinking water?

Yes. Unlike classic ion-exchange softeners, TESL® II removes nothing from the water. You keep drinking mineral-rich, healthy water, just without the limescale problems.

How much effort is the installation of the water filter?

A professional installer carries out the fitting and it is usually done quickly. After that the system works in the background with little maintenance, very low power consumption from the electromagnets, no refilling, no effort.

Which TESL® II model suits which household size?

TESL® II MINI is ideal for apartments and individual appliances, MEDIUM for single- and multi-family homes, MAX for commerce, industry and agriculture. In the free consultation we find the right solution together.
